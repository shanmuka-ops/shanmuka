DIWALI 2024

This Diwali, I celebrated with my family in a warm and joyful way. We started the day by cleaning and decorating our home with vibrant rangoli, twinkling diyas, and colorful lights. In the evening, we performed a simple Lakshmi Puja together, sharing prayers and gratitude. Afterward, we enjoyed a delicious feast of homemade sweets like laddoos and savory snacks. The night was filled with laughter as we burst eco-friendly. Exchanging gifts and sharing stories made the festival even more special, strengthening our family bond. It was a perfect blend of tradition, love, and happiness!
